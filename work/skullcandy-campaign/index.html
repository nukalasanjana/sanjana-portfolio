01 / Overview

The Brief &
Context

This project tasked us with developing a full advertising campaign for Skullcandy — the audio brand founded in 2003 in Park City, Utah, originally built for the outdoor action sports demographic and since expanded into the premium audio market.

The process moved from brand research through consumer insight, creative strategy, and ultimately a full suite of campaign deliverables — manifesto, tagline, video spot, billboard, social, branded content, partnerships, and IRL activations.

Our research pointed to one consistent finding: Skullcandy listeners are primarily attracted to the bass. That became the thread the entire campaign is built on.

The Brand

Bold visual identity, powerful bass, and a culture rooted in music, movement, and unapologetic self-expression.

The Challenge

Avoid the generic "music for everyone" angle. Skullcandy's power is in who it's not for — finding that edge and making it the campaign's center of gravity.

02 / Brainstorming

Brand Truth,
Strategy & Big Idea

Before any creative, we worked through a brand truth, a focused creative strategy, and a central idea that the whole campaign could stand on.

Fact

Skullcandy is known for its powerful bass, creating a deep and immersive listening experience.

Observation

People who don't like Skullcandy find the bass too intense.

Insight

Skullcandy is only for the ones that can handle it.

Convince

"All or Nothing" Types

That

With Skullcandy, you don't have to settle for an ordinary listening experience.

Because

Skullcandy creates a listening experience that matches your intensity.

Support

Skullcandy headphones have a strong, immersive bass fit for the bold, intense, and extreme — you can feel it in your bones.

Idea Statement

Let's unleash your intensity.

You deserve not just to listen to music, but to feel and embody it. Skullcandy is made for people who want to live life bolder and louder — more bass, more color, more expression.

Campaign Tagline

Live Life on Full Blast.
